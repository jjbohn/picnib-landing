Two types of storage to save you money

Active Storage

This storage is for the projects you are currently working on. Our active storage allows for immediate, fast downloads of all your work.

When you're all done with a project, move it into cold storage for safe keeping and start saving some serious money (cold storage is 1/10th the price of active storage). If you need to access these albums, you can move them into active storage at anytime (retrieval can take up to three hours).
